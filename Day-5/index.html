What is safe social distancing?

If you can stay or work from home, you
should stay home.

If we want to slow the spread of COVID-19, we need to follow the
law, socially distance and maintain good hygiene

For businesses

  • Reguarly wash hands with soap and touch fewer surfaces
  • Cancel or avoid all non-essential errands, engagements and movements
  • Keep 1.5 metres - at least two arm lengths away from others
  • Call in sick, self isolate and ring your doctors if feeling unwell
